The Mentor Stage

From ages 54 to 72

As a Mentor, you can continue the activities you pursued during your Builder phase and maintain your sources of income. However, your primary focus will shift from empire-building to sharing your wealth of experience and knowledge with the aim of providing guidance to those who require it the most.

  • You have a unique gift to share.

    Your greatest worth lies not in the mastery of tasks, but in your ability to navigate negative emotions, handle setbacks, cultivate resilience, gain a broader perspective, and exhibit courage.

  • Sometimes just listening is enough.

    In this fast-paced world, it's a precious and uncommon gift for others.

  • Start small, then expand.

    Begin your journey as a Mentor by taking small steps, then gradually deepen your commitment. There's no requirement to initiate your mentorship with an elaborate plan; you can progress towards becoming a fully established Mentor over time.
