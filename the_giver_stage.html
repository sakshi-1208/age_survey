The Giver Stage

From ages 54 to 72

Following your progression through the stages of Dreamer, Explorer, Builder, and Mentor, you can ultimately savor the rewards of your efforts. Whether it's spending quality time with your grandchildren, embarking on adventurous journeys, or supporting a cause dear to your heart, you can utilize your wisdom, enthusiasm, and financial resources to make the most of this phase in your life.

  • Remember yourself as a Dreamer.

    Reflect upon your childhood dreams and ambitions. Consider how you can rekindle those aspirations and engage with current Dreamers who share similar dreams.

  • You can find your purpose by dedicating yourself to a cause.

    Don't heed anyone who advises you to simply relax and relish the concluding chapter of your life. Make a conscious effort to seek out a sense of purpose and engage in meaningful pursuits.

  • Seek out connections with Dreamers and Explorers.

    Participate in an intergenerational writing program, music class, or any other activity that enables you to engage meaningfully with younger individuals.
