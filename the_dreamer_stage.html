The Dreamer Stage

From birth to age 18

During this phase, your objective is to expand upon your aspirations and construct comprehensive plans for their realization. Emphasize the steps and actions required, rather than fixating solely on the end result, as this is the cornerstone for crafting a vision with a genuine chance of materializing.

  • List three captivating visions for the future.

    Please list three ambitious future aspirations that are intricate, well-defined, and encompass the necessary action plans to achieve them. It's acceptable if your eventual path leads you in an entirely different direction.

  • Interview role models

    Connect with individuals who are currently pursuing the same path you aspire to. Conduct interviews with them and integrate the insights you gain into your dream-mapping process.

  • Find the right mentor

    Instead of contacting highly accomplished individuals, approach those you admire and who are willing to offer guidance as you embark on your journey.
