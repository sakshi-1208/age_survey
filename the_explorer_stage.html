The explorer Stage

From ages 18 to 36

Now is the moment to dedicate yourself to a thorough exploration that will guide you to the specific domain where you will truly excel and feel the most passionate. Avoid merely accepting jobs out of a sense of obligation or societal expectations; doing so will only cause you to miss out on the opportunities that come with youth.

  • Exhaust each dream before moving on to the next.

    Providing a lackluster effort and then quickly concluding that a pursuit isn't suitable for you won't suffice. It's essential to fully explore the potential of each of your dreams, even if it requires several years of commitment and dedication.

  • Take risks.

    As someone in the exploration phase, you have relatively less at stake. Begin taking more risks to acquire the experience of pushing beyond your comfort zone.
